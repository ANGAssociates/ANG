AI in Pharma Switzerland

Switzerland, and particularly Basel, is one of the world’s leading hubs for pharmaceutical innovation. With global leaders like Roche and Novartis headquartered in Basel, the region is now at the forefront of Artificial Intelligence (AI) adoption across the pharmaceutical value chain.

From drug discovery to commercial operations, AI and Generative AI are reshaping how Life Sciences organizations operate, innovate, and deliver value.
